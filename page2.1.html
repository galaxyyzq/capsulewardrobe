Minnie is an interactive hanger that helps its owner building and maintaining a capsule wardrobe.

Weather Information Prompt
When opening your wardrobe, you will hear the music indicating the weather from Minnie.

Times of Wearing Prompt
Holding one Minnie, you will hear a piece of music. The pitch of the music indicates the times of wearing these clothes during this week. When the times of wearing goes higher, the pitch of the music goes higher.

Recycling Prompt
Minnie will remind you to recycle when you are trying to add a new piece of clothes.
